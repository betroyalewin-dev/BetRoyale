Quick FAQ

The questions players ask before they trust the queue.

How does BetRoyale decide who won?

We pull the winner straight from Supercell's API data after the friendly ends. Nobody self-reports anything.

Why do I need to verify my account?

Because burner accounts ruin wagering. We check email and Clash Royale details before you can jump in.

How do deposits and payouts work?

Stripe handles checkout when you buy balance, and Stripe payout onboarding sends cash outs back to your connected account.

Can I join without my player tag and friend link?

No. Your opponent needs a real way to add you, and we need the right account info to verify the match.

Who built BetRoyale?

BetRoyale was built by a UCLA student who was tired of friendly wagers being settled with screenshots, excuses, and chaos.
