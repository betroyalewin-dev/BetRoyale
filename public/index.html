Quick FAQ

The questions players ask before they trust the queue.

How does BetRoyale decide who won?

BetRoyale checks the Clash Royale friendly battle result after the duel. Players do not self-report the outcome.

Why do I need to verify my account?

Email verification and saved Clash Royale details reduce fake accounts and stop unverified users from entering the queue.

How do deposits and payouts work?

Deposits use Stripe checkout. Cash outs require Stripe payout onboarding, so funds can be sent back to your connected payout account.

Can I join without my player tag and friend link?

No. Both are required before queue entry so opponents can connect and the match can be verified correctly.

Who built BetRoyale?

BetRoyale was founded by a UCLA student and fellow Clash Royale player who wanted a fair, automated way to wager on friendly duels.
